Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Hotel Italia Cuenca respects your right to control your personal data. You may request the deletion of all data associated with your WhatsApp interactions with our reservation assistant at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion through any of the following methods:

Option 1: WhatsApp
Send a message saying "Delete my data" or "Eliminar mis datos" to our WhatsApp number: +1 (347) 587-9088.
Option 2: Email
Send an email to gerencia@hotelitalia.com.ec with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Include the phone number associated with your WhatsApp account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete:

  • Your conversation history with our WhatsApp assistant.
  • Your contact information from our conversation tracking system.
  • Your lead information from our internal records.
  • Any follow-up scheduling data associated with your phone number.

What Data May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained as required by law or legitimate business needs:

  • Completed reservation records: if you completed a booking, reservation records may be retained as required by Ecuadorian tax and hospitality regulations.
  • Payment records: transaction records processed through Stripe are subject to Stripe's data retention policies and financial regulations.

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation message once your data has been deleted.

Contact Us

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at:

  • Email: gerencia@hotelitalia.com.ec
  • WhatsApp: +1 (347) 587-9088
  • Address: Hotel Italia, Cuenca, Ecuador
