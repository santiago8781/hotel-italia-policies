Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Hotel Italia Cuenca ("we", "us", or "our") operates a WhatsApp-based reservation assistant for our hotel properties in Cuenca, Ecuador. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our WhatsApp service.

1. Information We Collect

When you message us on WhatsApp, we may collect:

  • Contact information: your WhatsApp phone number and profile name.
  • Message content: text messages, images, and documents you send us related to your reservation inquiry.
  • Reservation details: check-in/check-out dates, number of guests, room preferences, and quoted prices discussed during the conversation.
  • Payment information: we use Stripe as our payment processor. We do not store credit card numbers. Stripe handles all payment data under their own privacy policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your reservation inquiries and provide room availability and pricing.
  • Process and confirm hotel reservations.
  • Send follow-up messages about your inquiry if you have not completed a booking.
  • Generate internal reports to improve our service quality.
  • Communicate important information about your stay.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Conversation data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (cloud infrastructure).
  • Reservation data is managed through CloudBeds, our Property Management System.
  • All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized hotel staff.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our reservation assistant:

  • Meta / WhatsApp Business Platform: messaging infrastructure.
  • Google Gemini: AI-powered conversation processing (messages are processed but not used to train AI models).
  • CloudBeds: hotel property management and reservations.
  • Stripe: secure payment processing.
  • Google Sheets: internal lead tracking.

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

  • Conversation history is retained for up to 12 months after your last interaction.
  • Reservation records are retained as required by Ecuadorian law and hotel industry regulations.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Data Deletion).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of follow-up messages at any time by replying "STOP" or "NO" on WhatsApp.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
